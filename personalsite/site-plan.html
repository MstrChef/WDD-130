Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site is to provide new and old destiny players, with an understanding of how to complete the end game content without having to watch a 2 hour video. We help new players enjoy the end game content instead of missing out.

Audience

My audience would be people of all ages who play destiny or have an interest in the activities that the game has.
